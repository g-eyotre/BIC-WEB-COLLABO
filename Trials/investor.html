Caleb Akandwanaho Salim Saleh “Oriba” (Rtd), the Chief Coordinator of Operation Wealth Creation to rebrand Africa’s Upper Nile Valley from an area known for the vicious cycle of conflict, forced migration to a virtuous cycle of peace-building, flourishing cross border trade, investment and infrastructure development.
Below is a list of SMEs or startup ventures that were profiled during the event spanning various sectors so as to facilitate the provision of matchmaking services with potential clients, partners or investors.
